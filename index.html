Nowa Sól (New Salt)

My hometown, where I was born and raised. Let's face it, you've never heard of it!

Well what are the best things this city is known for?

The biggest garden gnomes are located here!

They are placed in a Gnome Park. Apart from this huge couple, there is a playground for kids and some wild animals such as kangoroos, deers or even lamas!

I know it's bold, but trust me - Pizzeria Maurizio serves one of THE BEST pizzas!

It's not thick, but they do put a LOT of garlic on it that doesnt' burn your mouth right away, but makes pizza taste phenomenally!

A very popular polish senator used to be a president of Nowa Sól.

He's very straight-forward and isn't afraid to criticize the government.

Here's some basic info about my hometown!

  • Founded: around XVI century

  • City Law: 1743

  • Surface: 21,8 square kilometers

  • Population: 38 566
